# Point of inflection

Change of concavity ([Convex Function](Convex%20Function.md) / [Concave Function](Concave%20Function.md))

$$
x\_{1}\<x\_{2}\implies f(x\_{1}) \< f(x\_{2})
$$

Equiv. $f^{\prime}\left(x\right)=0$ (necessary but insufficient)
