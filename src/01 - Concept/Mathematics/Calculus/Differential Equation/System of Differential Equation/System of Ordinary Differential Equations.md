# System of Ordinary Differential Equations

(First-order)

$$
y\_{i}^{\prime}=f_i\left(t,y\_{1},\dots,y\_{n}\right)
$$

for $i=1,\dots,n$

Alternatively, $\mathbf{y}^{\prime}=\mathbf{f}(t, \mathbf{y})$.

Initial condition: $\mathbf{y}(x\_{0})$
