# Continuity

* Continuous at $a$: $\displaystyle\lim\_{x\to a} f(x)=f(a)$;
  * $f(a)$ is defined,
  * $\displaystyle\lim\_{x\to a} f(x)$ exists.
* Continuous on $\left(a,b\right)$: Continuous on $x_0\in\left(a,b\right)$.
