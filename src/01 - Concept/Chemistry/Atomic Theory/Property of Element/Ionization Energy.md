# Ionization Energy

* $\ce{X(g) -> X+(g) + e-}; \Delta H=IE\_{1}$
* $\ce{X+(g) -> X^{2+}(g) + e-}; \Delta H=IE\_{2}$
* $\displaystyle\propto Z\_\\text{eff}$
  * IIA > IIIA (full-fill $s$-orbital)
  * VA > VIA (half-fill $p$-orbital)
