# Nucleophile

$\ce{Rad.}$ (w/ unpaired electron, generally formal charge = 0)

* High energy, can result in another radical
* $\ce{X.}$, $\ce{HO.}$, $\ce{RO.}$, $\ce{R.}$
* $\ce{A-B -> A. + B.}$
* Note: Hydrogen radical is unpreferred in organic chemistry
