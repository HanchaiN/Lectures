* $W=\int P \delta V = P\Delta V=nR\Delta T$
* $\Delta U=C\_{V}nR\Delta T$
* $Q=\Delta U + W = \left(C\_{V}+1\right)nR\Delta T=nC\_{P}\Delta T$ (Hence, the name molar heat capacity at constant pressure $C\_{P}$)
* $C\_{P}=C\_{V}+R$
