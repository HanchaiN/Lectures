# Enthalpy

$H=U+PV$: Measure of heat (for isobaric process)

$$
\\begin{align\*}
dH
&= dU+d(PV)\newline
&= dU+PdV\newline
&= dU+\delta W\newline
&= \delta Q
\\end{align\*}
$$
