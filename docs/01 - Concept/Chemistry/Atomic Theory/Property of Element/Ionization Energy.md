# Ionization Energy

- $\ce{X(g) -> X+(g) + e-}; \Delta H=IE_{1}$
- $\ce{X+(g) -> X^{2+}(g) + e-}; \Delta H=IE_{2}$
- $\displaystyle\propto Z_\text{eff}$
	- IIA > IIIA (full-fill $s$-orbital)
	- VA > VIA (half-fill $p$-orbital)
