# Continuity

- Continuous at $a$: $\lim\limits_{x\to a} f(x)=f(a)$;
	- $f(a)$ is defined,
	- $\lim\limits_{x\to a} f(x)$ exists.
- Continuous on $\left(a,b\right)$: Continuous on $x_0\in\left(a,b\right)$.
