# Isobaric Process

$$
dP=0
$$

- $W=\int P \delta V = P\Delta V=nR\Delta T$
- $\Delta U=C_{V}nR\Delta T$
- $Q=\Delta U + W = \left(C_{V}+1\right)nR\Delta T=nC_{P}\Delta T$ (Hence, the name molar heat capacity at constant pressure $C_{P}$)
- $C_{P}=C_{V}+R$
