# Enthalpy

$H=U+PV$: Measure of heat (for [[Isobaric Process]])

$$
\begin{align*}
dH
&= dU+d(PV)\newline
&= dU+PdV\newline
&= dU+\delta W\newline
&= \delta Q
\end{align*}
$$
