# Linear Transformation

- $f\left(u+v\right)=f\left(u\right)+f\left(v\right)$
- $f\left(c\cdot u\right)=c \cdot f\left(u\right)$

Therefore,

- $f\left(c_{1}u_{1}+\dots c_{n}u_{n}\right)=c_{1}f\left(u_{1}\right)+\dots+c_{n}f\left(u_{n}\right)$
- $f\left(0\right)=0$
