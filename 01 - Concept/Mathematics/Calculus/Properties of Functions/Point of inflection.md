# Point of inflection

Change of concavity ([[Convex Function]] / [[Concave Function]])

$$
x_{1}<x_{2}\implies f(x_{1}) < f(x_{2})
$$

Equiv. $f^{\prime}\left(x\right)=0$ (necessary but insufficient)
