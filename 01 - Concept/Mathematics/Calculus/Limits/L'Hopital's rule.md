# L'Hôpital's rule

$$
\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\frac{0}{0},\frac{\infty}{\infty}\implies\lim_{x\to a}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\lim_{x\to a}\frac{f^{\prime}(x)}{g^{\prime}(x)}
$$
