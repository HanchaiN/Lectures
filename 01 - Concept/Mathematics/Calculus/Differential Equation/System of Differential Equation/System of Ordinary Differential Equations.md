# System of Ordinary Differential Equations

(First-order)

$$
y_{i}=f_i\left(t,y_{1},\dots,y_{n}\right)
$$

for $i=1,\dots,n$

Alternatively, $\mathbf{y}^{\prime}=\mathbf{f}(t, \mathbf{y})$.

Initial condition: $\mathbf{y}(x_{0})$
