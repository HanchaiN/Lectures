# Decreasing Function

$$
x_{1}<x_{2}\implies f(x_{1}) \ge f(x_{2})
$$

Equiv. $f^{\prime}\left(x\right)\le0$

For strictly-decreasing function,

$$
x_{1}<x_{2}\implies f(x_{1}) > f(x_{2})
$$

Equiv. $f^{\prime}\left(x\right)<0$ (sufficient but not necessary)
