# Nucleophile

$\ce{Nu^{\ominus}}$ / $\ce{Nu^{\ast}}$ (w/ lone electron pair)

- Can react with positively charged carbons or electrophiles
- $\ce{HO^{\ominus}}$, $\ce{R^{\ominus}}$, $\ce{RO^{\ominus}}$, $\ce{CN^{\ominus}}$
- $\ce{H2O^{\ast}}$, $\ce{RO^{\ast}H}$, $\ce{N^{\ast}H3}$, , $\ce{RN^{\ast}H2}$
