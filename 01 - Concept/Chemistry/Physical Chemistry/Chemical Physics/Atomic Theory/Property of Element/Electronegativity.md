# Electronegativity

- Tendency to attract bonding electron
  - $\Delta \chi < 1.7$: [[Covalent Bonding]]
  - $\Delta \chi > 1.7$: [[Ionic Bonding]]
- $\propto Z_\text{eff}$
- $\chi_{\ce{F}}>\chi_{\ce{O}}>\chi_{\ce{N}}\approx\chi_{\ce{Cl}}>\chi_{\ce{Br}}>\chi_{\ce{S}}\approx\chi_{\ce{C}}$
